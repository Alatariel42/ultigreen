Reducing your Transportation Carbon Footprint

There are a multitude of ways to decrease your transportation carbon footprint! These include:

  • Carpool with coworkers

    In addition to reducing transportation emissions of you and a few coworkers by up to 75%, being the driver of a carpool entitles you to one free tank of gas per month. If you're interested in being in a carpool with coworkers, you can fill out and submit this form:
    Download Carpool Registration Form

  • Bike to work if within a reasonable radius

    Biking to work not only has zero emissions, but it is great exercise that can help jumpstart your day!

  • Take public transportation

    Traveling by bus uses only 2/3 of the emissions of cars, and traveling by train has just between 1/5 and 1/3 of the emissions. These statistics assume that only 1/4 of bus seats are filled, so the potential for reducing emissions is much higher with increased public transportation use.
